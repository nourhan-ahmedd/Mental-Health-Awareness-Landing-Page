Supportive Mental Health Resources

NAMI

NAMI is a well-known organization dedicated to providing support, education, and advocacy for individuals and families affected by mental illness.

7cups

7 Cups is an online platform that connects individuals with trained listeners for emotional support and anonymous chat. They offer a free peer support chat service.

ADAA

ADAA is a nonprofit organization dedicated to helping people with anxiety disorders and depression. They provide a collection of articles, self-help resources, and a therapist finder tool.

The Mighty

The Mighty is a community-driven platform where individuals share their mental health stories and experiences. It offers a supportive environment for connecting with others going through similar challenges.
